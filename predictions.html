A Note on Predictions

These predictions represent educated speculation based on current scientific understanding, technological trends, and expert analysis. Many factors could accelerate or prevent these developments - including technological breakthroughs, policy decisions, resource availability, and unforeseen events.

The probability percentages indicate relative likelihood based on current trajectories, not certainties. The future remains fundamentally uncertain and shaped by choices we make today. Our predictions about the far future are particularly speculative and should be viewed as possibilities rather than inevitabilities.

Remember: The future is not predetermined. Through conscious effort, wise governance, and technological responsibility, humanity can steer toward positive outcomes while avoiding catastrophic risks.
